  SELECT c.CustomerId, c.Givenname, c.Surname, d.AccountId, a.AccountId, d.Type FROM Customers c
  JOIN Dispositions d on d.CustomerId = c.CustomerId
  JOIN Accounts a on a.AccountId = d.AccountId
  WHERE a.AccountId = d.AccountId
  
  Each account can have many users, one owner and several disponents.
